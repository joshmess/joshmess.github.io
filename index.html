Booz Allen Hamilton

Cyber Intern (6/2020-8/2020)

During Summer of 2020, I was a Cyber Intern on the Malware Team for Booz Allen. Over 8 weeks with the firm, I developed a command-line tool that tags and searches potential malware samples based on numerous features. I also developed an application that, using a trained ML model, classifies a new potential malware sample. Both tools are a part of the firms adaptive malware analysis system. I will be returning to Booz Allen for Summer 2021.

UGA Small Satellite Research Lab

Mission Operations Team Lead (11/2020-Present)

The UGA SSRL has funding for two CubeSat missions from NASA ad the Air Force. As Undergraduate Lead for the Mission Operations Team, I oversee a team of 12 undergraduate STEM students. I am responsible for the development and maintenance of the UGA's own Ground Station System, which allows us to communicate with our system in low-earth-orbit. I also coordinate recruitment and onboarding as well as licensing with NOAA for our missions.
